About NFT

This piece was created by aggregating a ton of your submissions into one big render. I asked you all what you'd like to have included in a Twitter post, and complied all that I could manage over an x day period into this.

This is the very first piece in an ongoing collection around community projects, where I try and get everyone involved, so keep an eye out!

Collaborators

    Who am I?

    Hey! I'm Eden, a 16-year-old 3d artist from New Zealand, who loves to think up and implement new ideas and concepts. This is something I've only discovered recently, and it's mainly due to the NFT space that I've realised this.

    I created my Twitter account after wanting to post my work when I was still a beginner, and then I saw quite a few artists that I look up to showing interest and from there it snowballed. Fast forward to the present, it's been the biggest change in my life so far. I've discovered so many new traits and quirks about me and have developed as a person immensely.

    Apart from that, I enjoy a little coding, music, electrical engineering, video production and photography. But there's almost nothing I'm not interested in, to a degree!
